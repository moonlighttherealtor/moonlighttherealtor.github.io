Meet Moonlight

I’m more than just a Realtor — I’m a dedicated guide, an advocate for your goals, and a trusted partner on one of the most meaningful journeys of your life.

I’ve built my real estate practice on one key value: relationships first. To me, buying or selling a home is not just a transaction — it’s a transition. A new beginning. A life decision that deserves care, clarity, and attention to detail.

Based in Vancouver, I specialize in helping clients from all walks of life — from first-time buyers to seasoned investors — make confident real estate moves. With a deep understanding of our local market and a proactive approach to communication, I simplify the process so you can focus on what matters most: your next step forward.

Whether you’re buying a cozy condo, selling a family home, or seeking a long-term investment, I’m here with honest advice, effective strategy, and thoughtful service tailored to your needs. No pressure, no rush — just clear guidance and consistent support.

If you’re ready to take the next step — whether it’s buying, selling, or just learning more about your options — I’m here to help.

Let’s talk. Let’s plan. Let’s move forward together.
